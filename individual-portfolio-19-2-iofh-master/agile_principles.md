 1. [Our highest priority is to satisfy the customer through early and continuous delivery of valuable software.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/first_principle)



 2. [Welcome changing requirements, even late in development. Agile processes harness change for the customer’s competitive advantage.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/second_principle)



 3. [Deliver working software frequently, from a couple of weeks to a couple of months, with a preference to the shorter timescale.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/third_principle)



 4. [Business people and developers must work together daily throughout the project.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/fourth_principle)



 5. [Build projects around motivated individuals. Give them the environment and support they need, and trust them to get the job done.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/fifth_principle)



 6. [The most efficient and effective method of conveying information to and within a development team is face-to-face conversation.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/sixth_principle)



 7. [Working software is the primary measure of progress.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/seventh_principle)



 8. [Agile processes promote sustainable development. The sponsors, developers, and users should be able to maintain a constant pace indefinitely.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/eighth_principle)


  9. [Continuous attention to technical excellence and good design enhances agility.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/ninth_principle)


 10. [Simplicity–the art of maximizing the amount of work not done–is essential.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/tenth_principle)



 11. [The best architectures, requirements, and designs emerge from self-organizing teams.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/eleventh_principle)



 12. [At regular intervals, the team reflects on how to become more effective, then tunes and adjusts its behavior accordingly.](/individual-portfolio-19-2-iofh/agile/twelfth_principle)
